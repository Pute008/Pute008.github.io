Which football team can you watch in England?

West Ham United

If you travel to England you should definitely watch a football match. West Ham United F.C plays their matches at London Stadium. West Ham has played in the Premier League for a total of 25 seasons.

London Stadium

This stadium is used by West Ham and is also England's national stadium. It was designed for the 2012 Summer Olympic and Summer Paralympics. So it is a multi-purpose stadium that can be used for many different sports. It has a capacity for around 70 000 people.
